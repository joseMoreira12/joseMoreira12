#staythefuckhome

Unfortunatly, due to the recent coronavírus plague the world is lock down to their houses. Although it sounded good at the beginning, it has become frustrating being stuck without seeing your close and love ones. With all this fuss in this last 3 months and the mandatory quarentine we’re living, there are various ways tou distract yourself. Every single day, I grab my guitar or bass and give myself into the music and I get lost for hours. As a self learner for years I’m in a point that what I like the most is to create new harmonies and melodies wich I’ve never heard before. I also listen to new artists that show up in my recomended on Spotify or Youtube so I can be even more inspired to create new songs. Most of my inspirations come from rock music, since Alice in Chains, Silverchair, Red Hot Chilli Peppers but I really also love digging into something more experimental like Buckethead, one of my greatest influences. Being closed, and playing so much can improve my skills, so I decided to record some of my creations and analyse them in here.

Even though I play guitar and bass for years, this experience brought me more wisdom and different techniques as well. Its also a good thing to have this little creations catalogued and saved like a song library, so I can always come back to this and remember certain riffs or melodies. This mashup of home recordings is shortened for the reason that I don’t always register them, therefore I chose 6 between all of them. Later in this quarentine, for my birthday I received a audio recording device (focusrite), which it would let me record something a little bit more professional. So to end this research I will show you one of my finalized songs. I used a tuning we don’t normally see (DADGAD) and recorded guitar and bass finishing and master it and on Ableton. I also did the artwork you are about to see, which will eventually be the cover of an EP I will eventually launch.
